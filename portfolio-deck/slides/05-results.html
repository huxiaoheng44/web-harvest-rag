BM25 wins on MULTIVAC — brand-heavy content ("MLC", "PEAQ", "GS1 Digital Link") rewards exact keyword match. Vector wins on NFCorpus — technical medical abstracts vs. colloquial queries create a vocabulary gap that only semantic search bridges. Hybrid beats the better of the two on both corpora, but only once tuned per-corpus. Neither method is a safe default.

Validation: this harness's BM25 recall@100 on NFCorpus (23.0%) lands within 2 points of BEIR's published official BM25 baseline (25.0%) — the low absolute numbers are a property of the dataset, not a bug in the implementation.
